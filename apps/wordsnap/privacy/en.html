Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

WordSnap ("the App") values your privacy and complies with applicable laws.

1Information We Collect

  • Searched English words and translations (stored locally on device only)
  • Learning progress data (flashcards, quiz scores)
  • App settings (language, theme)

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as name, email, or location.

2App Permissions

  • Internet (INTERNET): Used for word translation, ad display, and pronunciation lookup.

3Third-Party Services

  • Google Cloud Translation API: Search queries are sent to Google servers for translation.
  • Google AdMob: May collect device advertising ID for ad display. Personalized ads can be turned off in Settings. Google Privacy Policy →
  • Free Dictionary API: Used for English pronunciation and example sentences. No personal data is collected.

4Data Storage & Security

All learning data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to external servers. The app protects your data through the device's standard security mechanisms.

5Data Retention

Learning data is retained on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app. You can delete it anytime via Settings > Data Management.

6Your Rights

  • Delete search history, favorites, and cache via Settings > Data Management.
  • Opt out of personalized ads via the toggle in Settings > Personalized Ads.
  • For additional deletion requests, contact us at the email below.

7Children's Privacy

This app does not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will delete it immediately.
